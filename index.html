BIOGRAPHY

Purity is a passionate and innovative photographer with over 3 years of experience capturing compelling moments through the lens. Based in Nairobi, Kenya specializes in portrait, landscape, event and commercial photography. Blending technical precision with a creative eye to produce images that resonate deeply with clients and audiences alike. Known for their ability to tell stories through visuals, Purity has worked with the pryme events company,waridi events, Edwin Macharia family and H.E Monica Juma delivering high-quality images that align with unique visions and objectives. Their work has been featured in world economic forum and is recognized for it vibrant colors, emotional depth and innovative compositions. When not behind the camera, Purity enjoys exploring new destinations for inspiration, mentoring aspiring photographers or experimenting with post-production techniques. With a commitment to excellence and an ever-evolving artistic vision, Purity continues to transform everyday moments into timeless works of art.
